KNOW ME

I am a student of Information Management at UiTM Rembau, Negeri Sembilan. I am a motivated individual who values responsibility, teamwork and continuous learning and I am working towards building strong skills in managing and organizing information. I create with a heart rooted in nature, quiet moments and the people who keep me grounded. My surrondings, family and everyday experiences shape the way I think, feel and design. To me design is not just about how something looks, but how it connects. I am drawn to simplicity, soft details and thoughful storytelling that feels honest and human. This space is where I share my journey, my work and the ideas I continue to grow. Each piece reflects my process, my curiosity and my evolving voice as a student.
